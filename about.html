About Friends of Dahlia Heights Elementary

Friends of Dahlia Heights Elementary is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 920751156) aligning near-term relief with long-term pathways to housing and income through practical, transparent initiatives.

Approach

  • Human-centered: respect, safety, informed consent.
  • Evidence-based: data-informed design, outcome reporting.
  • Partnership-driven: MOUs with local agencies and employers.
